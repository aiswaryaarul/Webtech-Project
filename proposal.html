Project Proposal: Save the Bees!


What is this page about?


This page is dedicated to finding out more information on the declining bee population in the world. It will have information on the importance of bees, their contribution to human society, why they are declining, and how we can help Save the bees

Objective of the page?

This page is important because bees are really important for the world and if humans don't take action, we will live in a dystopian society

Target Audience


Ideally it would be everyone in the world but to narrow down the audience and cater to a specific group better, the target will be college students and young professionals who are interested in sustainability and science


Content on this page

  • Images of bees
  • Excerpts from scientific research and UN Reports as well as links to these for further reading
  • Youtube video for supplemental information from discover.com
  • Lists of tasks we can do to save the bees
  • Interactive map with bee population decline rates if possible
